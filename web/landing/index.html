AI-powered personal task intake

Turn messy thoughts into one portable task vault.

NoBrainFog captures fragmented ideas from Discord, Email, WeChat Work, or CLI, organizes them into structured Markdown tasks, and keeps the real data layer simple: one local todo.md file.
